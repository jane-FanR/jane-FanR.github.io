Awards

O'Neill brothers' dreams came true when Ireland, for the first time, met and fell in love
for the tenderness, flavour and love present in our meals, inherited from the matriarch Betty O'Neill: Our stake house was recognized as Meat of The Year,
the most prestigious award for restaurants in Ireland. And not only once but 5 times - consecutively, from 2010 to 2015 however, despite
the hiatus that followed, the long wait not only made it worth it but also brought tears of amazement when, consecutively (again) in 2021 and 2022 our steak house was granted with the ultimate award, that takes place in Paris, France - Le Gran Viande.
At the cerimony, the chef from the restaurant that is being evaluated is invited to cook
his traditional recipes in a chosen French restaurant, then inspected rigorously by 3 chefs before the final decision.

“ It is quite an achievement, definitely fruits of dedication and quality, seeded back in Wexford, thanks Ma.” - said a moved John , son of Betty O'Neill.
